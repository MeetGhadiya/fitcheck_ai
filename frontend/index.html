Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free tier really free forever?+

Yes — the free tier is free forever. You get 3 try-ons per day with no credit card required. Upgrade only when you need more.

How accurate is the AI try-on?+

Our AI achieves 98%+ fit accuracy using IDM-VTON, one of the most advanced virtual try-on models available. Results improve with a clear, well-lit full-body photo.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your dashboard — no questions asked, no cancellation fee. Your data is deleted within 30 days of cancellation.

Does it work for all body types?+

Yes. FitCheck AI is trained on diverse body types including slim, regular, athletic, plus-size and petite. Simply enter your body measurements for best results.
